We Turn Houses Into Homes

Founded in 2009, Furnish. started as a small workshop in Los Angeles with a simple belief: everyone deserves beautiful, well-crafted furniture that lasts a lifetime.

Today, we serve over 50,000 happy customers across the US, offering more than 1,200 curated pieces that blend timeless design with modern functionality. Every item in our collection is hand-selected by our team of interior designers and quality experts.

We partner with sustainable manufacturers who share our commitment to quality craftsmanship and environmental responsibility — because the best furniture shouldn't cost the planet.

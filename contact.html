Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often.

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express options are available at checkout for 1–2 day delivery.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be in original condition. Contact us to initiate a return.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes! White-glove delivery and assembly is available in select cities. Add it to your order at checkout.

Can I customize furniture colors or fabrics?

Many of our pieces offer customization options. Look for the "Customize" button on product pages or contact us directly.
